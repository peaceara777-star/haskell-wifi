### ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why do I need sudo/Admin privileges?**
A: WiFi management requires system-level access to network interfaces.

**Q: Does this work on macOS?**
A: Currently in development. Linux and Windows are fully supported.

**Q: Can I use this on Termux/Android?**
A: No, this requires a full Linux environment with NetworkManager.

**Q: How do I uninstall?**
A: Run `stack uninstall` or remove the binary from `~/.local/bin/`
